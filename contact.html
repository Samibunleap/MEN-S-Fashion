Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a message above.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Free standard shipping on all orders over $50.
What is your return policy?
We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn and in original packaging. Initiate your return via our portal and we'll send a prepaid label.
How do I find my size?
Our full size guide with chest, waist, and inseam measurements is available on every product page and via the footer link. When between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days. Duties and taxes may apply.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, our warehouse may have already started processing. Contact us immediately at orders@mensfashion.com.
Are your products sustainably made?
Sustainability is core to everything we do. We use ethically sourced natural fibres, partner with certified manufacturers, and are working toward fully carbon-neutral shipping by 2026.
